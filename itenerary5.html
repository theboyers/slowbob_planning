💡 Kalamazoo Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-September. AZO airport is small and easy.
  • Late May/early June weather: 55-78°F, mild with occasional rain — great disc golf conditions
  • AZO has limited direct flights — you may need to connect through Chicago or Detroit.
  • Flip City is a mandatory stop. One of the best courses in the world for 45+ years.
  • Base yourself in downtown Kalamazoo for brewery access and central driving routes.
  • Kalamazoo sits between Grand Rapids and Lansing — great central location for courses.
  • Bring water and bug spray for Michigan wooded courses.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
