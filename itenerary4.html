💡 Burlington Trip Tips

  • Best time: June-September for mountain weather. Fall colors in September are incredible.
  • Late May/early June weather: 50-72°F, mild — pack layers for mountain courses
  • BTV is a small, easy airport — you'll be on the road fast.
  • Brewster Ridge + Fox Run Meadows at Smugglers' Notch are a REQUIRED double-play.
  • Bring layers — Vermont mountain weather changes rapidly even in summer.
  • Courses are spread out — each direction is a 30-45 min drive from Burlington.
  • Book tee times for Brewster/Fox Run in advance during peak season.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
