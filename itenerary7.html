💡 Cleveland Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-September. CLE is a major airport with plenty of flight options.
  • Base yourself in Ohio City or downtown for brewery access and central location.
  • Punderson is the crown jewel — it's 45 min east from CLE but worth the drive.
  • Ohio City has 5 breweries within walking distance (Great Lakes, Market Garden, etc.).
  • Day 2 (Akron) covers many courses in the Akron/Canton corridor.
  • Many Cleveland courses are in Metroparks — check for park entry fees.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
