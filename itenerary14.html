💡 Sacramento Trip Tips

  • Best time: April-October for warm weather and long days
  • Shady Oaks is a must-play — Sacramento's highest-rated course
  • Summer temperatures can exceed 100°F — bring plenty of water
  • Sacramento has an excellent farm-to-fork food scene for dinners
  • The foothill courses (Cameron Park, Auburn) are significantly cooler in summer
  • SMF is a small, easy airport — very quick rental car pickup/dropoff
