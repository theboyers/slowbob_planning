💡 Boston Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-October for longest daylight and best weather
  • Maple Hill is an absolute must-play — top 10 course in the world
  • Boston traffic is intense — budget extra time and consider staying west of the city
  • Maple Hill requires advance tee time reservation — book early
  • Tree House Brewing in Charlton is a perfect post-Maple Hill dinner spot
  • Bring bug spray for wooded courses in spring/early summer
