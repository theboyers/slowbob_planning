💡 Grand Rapids Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-September. Fall is beautiful but Flip City is a must-play every trip.
  • Late May/early June weather: 55-75°F, mild with occasional rain — ideal disc golf temps
  • Base yourself near downtown GR for central access to all directions.
  • Flip City is 45 min SW — make the drive. It's been in the world top 100 for 45 years.
  • Kensington Toboggan is pro-level — only open in the summer months for the DGLO layout.
  • GRR airport is small and easy to navigate — you'll be on the course fast.
  • Bring water and bug spray — Michigan woods have mosquitoes in summer.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
