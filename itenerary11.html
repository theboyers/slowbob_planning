💡 Raleigh/Durham Trip Tips

  • Best time: March-May or September-October. Pleasant temperatures.
  • Late May/early June weather: 62-83°F, warm and humid — light rain gear recommended
  • RDU is conveniently located between Raleigh and Durham — fast access to all courses.
  • Research Triangle Park has a huge disc golf community.
  • Jones Park in Apex is the highest-rated course in the area.
  • Courses are well-distributed across Cary, Apex, Durham, and Raleigh.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
