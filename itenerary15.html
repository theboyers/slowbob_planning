💡 Salt Lake City Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-September for optimal weather and mountain access
  • Late May/early June weather: 55-78°F, mild and dry — ideal mountain disc golf
  • Creekside Park is a must-play — Utah's highest-rated course
  • SLC airport is very convenient — quick rental car pickup/dropoff
  • Altitude is ~4,300 ft — stay hydrated, discs fly more overstable
  • Utah liquor laws require food with alcohol at breweries — check hours
  • Mountain courses can be 10-15°F cooler than the valley
