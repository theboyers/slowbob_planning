💡 Atlanta Trip Tips

  • Best time: March-May or September-October. Summer is hot and humid.
  • Late May/early June weather: 68-86°F, hot and humid with afternoon storms — hydrate well
  • ATL is a massive hub — cheap direct flights from everywhere.
  • Perkerson Park and Central Park are both 15 min from ATL — perfect arrival day.
  • Atlanta traffic is brutal — play your course direction for the day, don't cross town.
  • Many courses are in parks that close at dusk — start early for 4 rounds.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
