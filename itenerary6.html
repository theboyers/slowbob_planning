💡 Madison Trip Tips

  • Best time: May-September. MSN airport is very close to the city center.
  • Late May/early June weather: 55-77°F, mild with occasional storms — pack a rain jacket
  • Madison's top-rated course (Token Creek) is 15 min from MSN — best airport-to-course of any option.
  • Wisconsin has 471 courses — Madison is a great basecamp for exploring the region.
  • Day 3 (Green Bay direction) requires a 2 hr drive each way — pack lunch for the road.
  • Consider flying into MSN and renting one vehicle for the group.
  • Bring bug spray — Wisconsin summer mosquitoes are relentless.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
