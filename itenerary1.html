💡 Portland Trip Tips

  • Best time: June-September for longest daylight (14-15 hrs) to fit 4 rounds/day
  • Late May/early June weather: 55-70°F, occasional rain — pack layers and rain gear
  • Milo McIver gets busy on weekends — Friday is ideal to avoid crowds
  • Blue Lake is 10 min from PDX — perfect for landing and playing immediately
  • Bring extra discs — Portland courses eat discs in water hazards (Blue Lake especially)
  • Download UDisc app for course maps, ratings, and live scoring
  • Bring rain gear even in summer — Pacific Northwest weather changes fast
