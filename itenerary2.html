💡 Kansas City Trip Tips

  • Best time: April-May or Sept-Oct for mild temps. Summer is hot and humid.
  • Late May/early June weather: 65-82°F, humid with thunderstorms possible — start early
  • MCI airport is far north — base yourself in midtown/Westport for central access
  • Water Works Park at sunset for epic downtown skyline views
  • Rosedale Up Top + Down Under are in the same park — perfect for back-to-back rounds
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring
  • Bring water — KC courses can have limited shade
