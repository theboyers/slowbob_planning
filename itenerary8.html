💡 Denver Trip Tips

  • Best time: June-September. Mountain courses are only in full swing during summer.
  • Late May/early June weather: 52-78°F, afternoon thunderstorms common — play early
  • Beaver Ranch + Bucksnort + Ghost Town are world-class — make Day 2 the mountain day.
  • Altitude adjustment: Denver is 5,280 ft. Mountain courses are 8,000+ ft. Drink water.
  • DIA is massive — factor in 15 min from gate to rental car.
  • Expect afternoon thunderstorms in summer — play early.
  • Download UDisc for course maps. Mountain courses are complex.
