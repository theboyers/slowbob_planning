💡 DFW Trip Tips

  • Best time: March-May or September-November. Summer is brutally hot.
  • Late May/early June weather: 70-88°F, hot with storm chances — check radar before heading out
  • DFW is a massive airport hub — cheap direct flights from everywhere.
  • Base yourself in Arlington or the Mid-Cities for central access to all courses.
  • DFW sprawl is real — pick a direction each day and stick to it.
  • Turner Park (15 min from DFW) is the closest top course to the airport.
  • Hydrate — Texas heat is no joke even in spring/fall.
  • Download UDisc for course maps and live scoring.
