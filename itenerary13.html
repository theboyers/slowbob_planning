💡 Seattle Trip Tips

  • Best time: July-September for longest daylight and best weather
  • Late May/early June weather: 52-68°F, mild with occasional drizzle — bring a light jacket
  • Lakewood is a must-play — Washington's highest-rated course
  • Traffic in Seattle is brutal — build in extra drive time between courses
  • Book SEA parking in advance if renting a car
  • Bring rain gear even in summer — Pacific Northwest weather changes fast
  • Ballard and Fremont have the best brewery concentration for post-round dinners
