💡 Austin Trip Tips

  • Best time: March-May or September-November. Summer is 100+ degrees.
  • Late May/early June weather: 72-90°F, hot — start early and stay hydrated
  • Roy G Guerrero is 15 min from AUS — best airport-to-course ratio of any option.
  • Start early every day to beat the Texas heat.
  • Hydrate constantly — Austin in summer is no joke.
  • AUS is a small, easy airport. You'll be on the course fast.
